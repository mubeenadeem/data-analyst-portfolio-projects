### 02 — HR Employee Attrition Analysis
> **Tools:** Python · Pandas · Matplotlib · Seaborn

Analyzed IBM HR dataset of 1,470 employees to find the key reasons why employees leave the company.

**Key Insights:**
- Overall attrition rate: **16.12%** (237 out of 1,470 employees left)
- Employees who left earned **$4,787/month** vs **$6,833** for those who stayed
- Employees doing overtime were **3x more likely to leave** (30.5% vs 10.4%)
- **R&D department** had the highest number of employees leaving
- Younger employees (avg age 33.6) left more than older ones (avg 37.6)
- Employees who left had stayed only **5.1 years** vs 7.4 years for those who stayed

**Recommendation:** Increase salaries, reduce overtime, and improve job satisfaction in R&D to reduce attrition.
